ABOUT US

Founded in 1968, the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at ACGCET is committed to developing skilled engineers ready to excel in a rapidly changing technological landscape. We emphasize academic rigor and personal growth through innovative programs that equip students with essential professional skills.
Our focus on research and collaboration encourages students to address contemporary technological challenges. A key initiative is SYNGELS, our inter-college symposium, which fosters intellectual engagement and competition.
