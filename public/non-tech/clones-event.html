GAME OF CLONES

Get ready for Game of clones the ultimate, fast-paced, and laugh-out-loud quiz experience designed for film fanatics! This thrilling challenge is packed with quirky and clever clues that will test your knowledge of movies, iconic characters, and unforgettable songs. Whether you're a casual viewer or a seasoned cinephile, you'll be racing against the clock to connect the dots and shout out your guesses before anyone else. It's not just about what you know, but how fast you can think! Perfect for friendly competition, Game of Clones brings the energy of the big screen to life, with fun, excitement, and epic bragging rights up for grabs. So, gather your friends, flex your film muscles, and prepare to dive into a whirlwind of cinematic trivia that’s sure to keep you on your toes!

ROUND 1
This round is all about connecting the dots using quirky and entertaining clues that will lead you straight to the films you know and love. Get ready for a mix of brief story descriptions, unique dialogues, iconic images, and memorable songs that will tickle your funny bone and test your movie knowledge!

ROUND 2
Get ready for Round 2 of Movie Mania, Rapid Fire Movie Madness! This round is all about speed, excitement, and quick thinking. You’ll have only a few seconds to answer each question, so keep your wits about you and be ready to shout out your answers before time runs out!

ROUND 3
This round introduces a competitive twist where participants can earn points in a tiered challenge that tests their movie knowledge in a fun and engaging way. Get ready to strategize, collaborate, and prove your cinematic prowess!
