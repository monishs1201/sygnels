CLASH OF CIRCUITS

This event tests participants' proficiency in designing circuits and projects using Arduino in Tinkercad. Each team will be given a real-world problem, and they must design an efficient project using various sensors and components in the Arduino environment. From wiring circuits to writing code, teams will need to ensure their project works flawlessly and meets the specified application requirements. The first team to complete their project with accurate functionality and output will be declared the winner. This is your chance to demonstrate innovation and practical problem-solving using Arduino technology!

ROUND 1
1. Each team should contain 2-3 members.
2. Join us for an exciting event where participants will embark on a lyrical tech- themed treasure hunt.

ROUND 2
1. Each team should be capable of analyzing and solving real-time problems efficiently.
2. Teams are required to simulate their circuit designs for validation.
3. The first team to successfully design and present the solution will be declared as the winner.
