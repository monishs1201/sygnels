BRAVO'S EXCHANGE

Deal Dash is an electrifying event that combines circuit-building skills with strategy and negotiation. Teams are provided with incomplete circuit kits, and to complete their circuit, they must acquire missing components from other teams. ROUND 1
1. Each team should contain 2-3 members.
2. A thrilling rapid-fire round where participants will be challenged and evaluated through an exciting tech-themed memory game.

ROUND 2
The teams will be given a circuit with a limited set of components. The teams will be negotiating with the other teams for the other components.
Trades hinge on quiz-style questions, where a correct answer blocks the exchange and a wrong one secures it.
Each negotiation will be for 7-10 minutes.
The first team to complete will be declared as the winner.
