WINTER EXPO

The Winter Expo is a showcase of innovation in Electronics and Communication Engineering, where students present cutting-edge projects and creative solutions. This event highlights technological advancements in various ECE domains, inspiring attendees with hands-on demonstrations and interactive displays. Join us to explore the future of engineering and celebrate student ingenuity.

RULES
1. Each team should contain 2-3 members.
2. The participants will be showcasing their projects and will be asked questions by the judges.
3. The judges' decision will be the final.
