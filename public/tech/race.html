CRYPTS OF SECRETS

Enter the cryptic world of codes and encryption in Decryption Race, where participants will solve puzzles related to cryptographic decryption techniques. Teams are tasked with finding the correct key to decode encrypted data using advanced cryptographic algorithms and methods. The fastest team to decrypt the message and retrieve the hidden key will win. This event not only tests your coding skills but also your knowledge of encryption standards and security protocols. Ready to unlock the secrets?

ROUND 1
1. Each team should consist of 2-3 members.
2. The participants will be provided with some simple and basic questions to test the logical skill as well as the basic knowledge in cryptography.

ROUND 2
1. Teams will participate in problem-solving tasks, solve puzzles, and search for hidden clues.
2. The primary goal is to find the key
3. After finding the key Participants must decrypt the given encrypted msg and finally find the plaintext by using the key and complete the challenge.
